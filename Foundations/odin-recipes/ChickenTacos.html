Chicken Tacos

Description

Chicken Tacos are a quick and easy meal that's perfect for any night of the week. Filled with seasoned chicken, fresh vegetables, and topped with your favorite salsa.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts
  • Olive Oil
  • Chili Powder
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper
  • Tortillas
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Shredded Cheese
  • Salsa
  • Sour Cream

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  2. Rub chicken breasts with olive oil and season with chili powder, garlic powder, onion powder, cumin, salt, and black pepper.
  3. Place seasoned chicken breasts on a baking sheet and bake in the preheated oven until cooked through, about 25 minutes. Let cool, then shred with a fork.
  4. Warm tortillas in a skillet over medium heat until soft.
  5. Assemble tacos by placing shredded chicken on each tortilla. Top with lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, salsa, and sour cream.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious chicken tacos.
