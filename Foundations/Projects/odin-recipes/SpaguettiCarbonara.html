Spaghetti Carbonara

Description

Spaghetti Carbonara is a classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and pepper. It's creamy, delicious, and easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti
  • Eggs
  • Parmesan Cheese
  • Pancetta
  • Garlic
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook spaghetti in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, about 9 minutes.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add pancetta and garlic; cook until pancetta is crispy and garlic is fragrant, about 5 minutes.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together eggs and grated Parmesan cheese until well combined.
  4. Drain spaghetti, reserving some pasta water. Quickly toss hot spaghetti in the skillet with pancetta and garlic.
  5. Remove skillet from heat and quickly pour in egg mixture, tossing constantly to create a creamy sauce. Add reserved pasta water as needed to reach desired consistency.
  6. Season with black pepper and salt to taste. Serve immediately with extra Parmesan cheese on top.
